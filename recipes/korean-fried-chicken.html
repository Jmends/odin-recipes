Korean Fried Chicken

Korean fried chicken that is a version of fried chicken found in korean. This recipe makes tender, juicy, and flavorful chicken and it is a must try!

Ingredients

    Marinade:

  • 1 pound skinless,boneless chicken thighs, quarted
  • 1/2 yellow onion, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground balck pepper

    Other:

  • 4 cups for frying, or as needed

    Batter:

  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup self-rising flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup very cold water, or as needed
